Sorghum Buddha bowl

  1. Step 1: Rinse the sorghum under cold water. In a medium saucepan, combine sorghum and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 50-60 minutes, or until sorghum is tender but still has a slight chew. Drain any excess water.
  2. Step 2: While the sorghum is cooking, prepare the dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Step 3: In a large bowl, combine cooked sorghum, chickpeas, kale, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  4. Step 4: Drizzle the prepared dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  5. Step 5: If using, sprinkle feta cheese on top.
  6. Step 6: Serve the Sorghum Buddha Bowl in individual bowls or plates.

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1: 1 cup sorghum
  • Ingredient 2: 2 cups water
  • Ingredient 3: 1 cup chickpeas, cooked
  • Ingredient 4: 2 cups kale, chopped
  • Ingredient 5: 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Ingredient 6: 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • Ingredient 7: 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Ingredient 8: 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • Ingredient 9: 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Ingredient 10: Salt and pepper to taste

Macros

  • Protein: 15 grams
  • Fat: 45 grams
  • Carbohydrates: 15 grams

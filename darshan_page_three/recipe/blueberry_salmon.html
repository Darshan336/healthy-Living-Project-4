Blueberry Salmon

  1. Step 1: In a small saucepan, combine the fresh blueberries, honey, and balsamic vinegar.
  2. Step 2: Simmer over low heat for about 10 minutes, or until the blueberries break down and the mixture thickens. Stir occasionally. Set aside.
  3. Step 3: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Step 4: Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or foil.
  5. Step 5: Brush each fillet with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Step 6: Spoon the prepared blueberry glaze over the salmon fillets, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  7. Step 7: Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  8. Step 8: Remove the salmon from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  9. Step 9: Garnish with fresh mint leaves before serving.

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1: 4 salmon fillets
  • Ingredient 2: 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Ingredient 3: 2 tablespoons honey
  • Ingredient 4: 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Ingredient 5: 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Ingredient 6: Salt and pepper to taste
  • Ingredient 7: Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Macros

  • Protein: 18 - 20 grams
  • Fat: 10 grams
  • Carbohydrates: 10-12 grams

Mediterranean Chicken

  1. Step 1: Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Step 2: In a small bowl, mix together the dried oregano, thyme, rosemary, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Step 3: Place the chicken breasts on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Drizzle olive oil over the chicken.
  4. Step 4: Sprinkle the herb mixture evenly over the chicken breasts, making sure to coat both sides.
  5. Step 5: Arrange lemon slices, cherry tomatoes, and Kalamata olives around the chicken on the baking sheet.
  6. Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165째F (74째C) and is cooked through.
  7. Step 7: Remove from the oven and sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the chicken.
  8. Step 8: Garnish with fresh parsley.
  9. Step 9: Serve hot, with any remaining roasted vegetables from the baking sheet.

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Ingredient 2: 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Ingredient 3: 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • Ingredient 4: 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Ingredient 5: 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Ingredient 6: 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Ingredient 7: Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Ingredient 8: 1 lemon, sliced
  • Ingredient 9: 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Ingredient 10: 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • Ingredient 11: 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • Ingredient 12: Fresh parsley for garnish

Macros

  • Protein: 30 grams
  • Fat: 15 grams
  • Carbohydrates: 10 grams

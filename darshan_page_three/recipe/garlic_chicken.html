Garlic Chicken

  1. Step 1: In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Step 2: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 30 seconds or until fragrant.
  3. Step 3: Add the marinated chicken to the skillet. Cook for 4-5 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Step 4: In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed. Add the sliced bell peppers, broccoli, and carrot. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Step 5: Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and toss with the vegetables. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to heat through.
  6. Step 6: Season with salt and pepper to taste and Serve hot, garnished with chopped green onions.

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1: 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Ingredient 2: 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Ingredient 3: 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Ingredient 4: 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Ingredient 5: 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Ingredient 6: 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Ingredient 7: 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • Ingredient 8: 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • Ingredient 9: 1 cup broccoli florets
  • Ingredient 10: 1 carrot, sliced
  • Ingredient 11: Salt and pepper to taste
  • Ingredient 12: Green onions, chopped for garnish

Macros

  • Protein: Approximately 25-30 grams
  • Fat: Approximately 15-20 grams
  • Carbohydrates: Approximately 10-15 grams

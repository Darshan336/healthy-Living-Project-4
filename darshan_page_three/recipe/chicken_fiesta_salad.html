Chicken Fiesta Salad

  1. Step 1: In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, cherry tomatoes, corn, black beans, red bell pepper, cucumber, avocado, red onion, and cilantro.
  2. Step 2: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, honey, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Step 3: Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to coat.
  4. Step 4: Top the salad with grilled and sliced chicken breast.
  5. Step 5: Serve with lime wedges on the side.

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1: 250g chicken breast, grilled and sliced
  • Ingredient 2: 2 cups mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • Ingredient 3: 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Ingredient 4: 1/4 cup corn kernels (cooked)
  • Ingredient 5: 1/4 cup black beans (canned, rinsed and drained)
  • Ingredient 6: 1/4 cup red bell pepper, chopped
  • Ingredient 7: 1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
  • Ingredient 8: 1/4 cup avocado, diced
  • Ingredient 9: 2 tablespoons red onion, finely chopped
  • Ingredient 10: 2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
  • Ingredient 11: 1 lime, cut into wedges (for garnish)

Macros

  • Protein: 25 - 30 grams
  • Fat: 10 - 15 grams
  • Carbohydrates: 15 - 20 grams

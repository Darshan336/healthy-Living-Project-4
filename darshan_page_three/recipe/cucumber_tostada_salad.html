Cucumber Tostada salad

  1. Step 1: In a large bowl, combine sliced cucumbers, halved cherry tomatoes, thinly sliced red onion, black beans, and corn kernels.
  2. Step 2: Squeeze the juice of one lime over the salad mixture. Add salt and pepper to taste. Toss to combine.
  3. Step 3: Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese and chopped fresh cilantro over the salad.
  4. Step 4: Gently mix all the ingredients to ensure an even distribution of flavors.
  5. Step 5: Arrange tostada shells on a plate. Spoon the salad mixture onto the tostada shells.

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1: 2 large cucumbers, sliced
  • Ingredient 2: 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Ingredient 3: 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Ingredient 4: 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • Ingredient 5: 1/4 cup corn kernels
  • Ingredient 6: 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • Ingredient 7: 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Ingredient 8: 2 tostada shells
  • Ingredient 9: Juice of 1 lime

Macros

  • Protein: 10-12 grams
  • Fat: 30-35 grams
  • Carbohydrates: 8-10 grams

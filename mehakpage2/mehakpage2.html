Biological Health

Men's health often relates to biological factors such as the male reproductive system or to conditions caused by hormones specific to, or most notable in, males.

PCOD

PCOD stands for Polycystic Ovarian Disease. It is one of the most common conditions that affect 5% to 10% of women who lie in the age group of 12 to 45 years.

PCOS

Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) is a problem with hormones that happens during the reproductive years. If you have PCOS, you may not have periods very often. Or you may have periods that last many days.
